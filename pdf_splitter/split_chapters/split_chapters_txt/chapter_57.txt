Chapter 57
CARPETS AND OTHER TEXTILE FLOOR COVERINGS
Notes.
1. For the purpose of this Chapter, the term "carpets and other textile floor coverings" means floor coverings in which textile
materials serve as the exposed surface of the article when in use and includes articles having the characteristics of textile
floor coverings but intended for use for other purposes.
2. This Chapter does not cover floor covering underlays.

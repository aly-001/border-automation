Chapter 81
OTHER BASE METALS; CERMETS; ARTICLES THEREOF

Supplementary Note.
1. In this Chapter the following expressions have the meanings hereby assigned to them:
(a) Not alloyed
Metallic substances containing by weight at least 99% of the respective base metal.
(b) Alloyed
Metallic substances in which the respective base metal predominates by weight over each of the other elements provided
that the total content by weight of such other elements exceeds 1%.
